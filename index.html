About Us

Taufique Steel and Tasleem Steel were founded by Mr. Muhammad Taufique and Mr. Muhammad Tasleem respectively and supplying good quality Galvanized Steel Sheets for the past 36 years.

Taufique Steel and Tasleem Steel are well known and highly reputed firms in the market of plain & corrugated galvanized steel sheets throughout Pakistan.

We are Members of:

  • Karachi Chamber of Commerce and Industry (KCCI)
  • Karachi Iron and Steel Merchants Association (KISMA)
  • Red Crescent

We are Dealer of Pakistan Steel Mills.

Services

Featured Services

Steel Coils Sheering

We offer expert steel coil shearing services, capable of cutting H.R., C.R., E.G., and G.I. coils without damaging your material. Our skilled team ensures minimal wastage and top-notch precision.

Bending Of Steel Sheets

We also provide steel sheet bending services to our customers. Maximum length: Upto 22 ft Thickness range: Upto 5 mm Material: GI, PPGI, CR, HR and EG Sheets

Profile Corrugation

We provide classic design profile corrugation services to our customers who wants their raw material to be profile corrugated

Hole Punching In Steel Sheets

We offer hole punching facility to our customers on their steel sheets.

Container Style Corrugation

We also corrugate steel sheets into container design. We also give the facility of choosing their own design to our customers.

Cutting Of Steel Sheets For Ice Box

We are specialist in cutting steel coils for GI Steel Ice Boxes with minimum wastage. Thus, ensuring maximum value of money for our customers.
